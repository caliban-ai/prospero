# ####. Short title of the decision

- **Status:** Proposed | Accepted | Deprecated | Superseded by [####](####-topic.md)
- **Date:** YYYY-MM-DD
- **Deciders:** who made the call (names / roles)

## Context

What is the problem or force that requires a decision? Describe the situation,
constraints, and options considered — enough that a reader who wasn't there understands
*why a decision was needed*. State the alternatives you weighed.

## Decision

The decision, in the active voice: "We will …". Be specific about what is and isn't
included. Link to the relevant design doc, spec, or code if one exists.

## Consequences

What becomes easier and what becomes harder as a result — positive, negative, and
neutral. Call out new constraints this imposes, follow-up work it implies, and anything
it deliberately leaves open. This is where you record the trade-off you accepted.
